About Me

Hey there! I'm Katz, a freelance developer with services primarily centered around Discord. I began learning to code in around 2012. I joined Discord mid-2018 and began developing bots about a year after that. I started my journey as a moderator for numerous servers with some of the most notable being Dank Memer Support & Community and Members+.

After some time working on content moderation, I naturally shifted towards management roles, including running events and/or backend operations and working at discords.com. While running these servers and exploring more, I noticed the need for community-specific integrations.

I now mainly focus on the development side of servers and create and manage bots for numerous large communities. Check out my Portfolio page for more details on past work or my Projects page for personal projects I'm working on. Thanks for checking out my site!

Commissions

I currently offer commissions for bots and websites. If there is something else that you need that may be related to my experience, please reach out and I'll do my best to assist you. My pricing varies greatly based exclusively on what you need done, how elaborate it is, and how many revisions you want made. There are no cookie cutter package prices for any of my services, and it is entirely tailored based on your needs.

To get in contact with me, please send me an e-mail or direct message. Sending multiple messages or using both modes of contact is not necessary.
